Accessibility statement

Last updated — 2026-07-01

We want Elevé to be usable by everyone and are working toward WCAG 2.1 AA.

What we have done

  • Visible keyboard focus states and a global reduced-motion setting (prefers-reduced-motion).
  • Balanced, legible typography and sufficient text contrast on light backgrounds.
  • Sound effects are muteable, and motion respects the OS reduced-motion preference.

Known limitations (being addressed)

  • The custom cursor and some 3D/canvas interactions (room builder, cube scene) are not yet fully keyboard- or screen-reader-operable.
  • A full third-party audit and screen-reader test pass is planned.

Feedback

If you hit a barrier, email creative@sentientbyelysian.com and we will help and prioritise a fix.
