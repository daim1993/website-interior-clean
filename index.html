The founder / 04

The eye behind Elevé

CD

Camille Devereux

Founder · Lead Interior Designer

Camille founded Elevé on a simple conviction: a room should feel inevitable — as if it could not have been arranged any other way. Trained in architecture in Paris and Lisbon, she spent a decade designing residences, ateliers and hospitality spaces before bringing that sensibility under one studio.

Her work is quiet and material-led: honest stone, warm timber, considered light, and just enough emptiness to let a space breathe. She leads every project personally, from first sketch to final styling.
